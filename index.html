Lanka Software Foundation (LSF) is a Non-governmental non profit organization to support technical upgradation of Sri Lanka's public infrastructure and beyond to the global governments by helping digital solution designing, and open-source community's works.

Starting from our "Get Involved" page would be ideal. Here you can choose the current project you are most interested in and can handle from a list. We welcome hobbyists as well as developers, scholars, legislators, and more.

LSF encourages the incubation of open-source projects which are innovative in nature and predominantly serve digital governance, public infrastructure, e-government, digital identity, finance, etc.

LSF offers mentorship and internship programs that provide professional guidance and hands-on exposure along with certain other advantages such as networking and career development in technology and open-source domains.

LSF has a worldwide view of the collaborations like governments, international institutions, universities, private-sector technology entrepreneurs, and open-source communities working together on cutting-edge digital transformation projects.
